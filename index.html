Questions
FAQ
How do I download a YouTube video as MP4?
+
Paste the YouTube URL, make sure MP4 is selected, then click Fetch → Download. The file saves with the original title.
Can I extract MP3 audio from YouTube?
+
Yes. Toggle Format to MP3, paste the link and download. Best mode uses ffmpeg to convert the best available audio to high-quality MP3.
Which platforms are supported?
+
1000+ sites including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, Vimeo, Reddit, Twitch, Dailymotion, SoundCloud, Bilibili and more — powered by yt-dlp.
Is it completely free?
+
Yes, 100% free. No account, no watermarks, no subscription, no ads in your downloads.
My MP4 has no video — only audio. Why?
+
Switch to Best mode — it forces yt-dlp to download a real video stream and merge it with ffmpeg. Direct/Other may pick audio-only streams.
What's the maximum quality available?
+
Up to 4K (2160p) for platforms like YouTube that offer it. Use Best mode for the highest available resolution.
Can I add this to my phone's home screen?
+
Yes! On Android tap the browser menu → "Add to Home screen". On iPhone open Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen".
