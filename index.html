Getting started
How to download a video
Four steps, under 30 seconds. No account, no app, no watermarks.
01

Copy the video link

Go to YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter or any supported platform. Click Share → Copy Link. You can also right-click and copy the URL from the address bar.

02

Choose format and engine

Toggle MP4 for video or MP3 for audio-only. Select your engine: Best for highest quality, Direct for speed, or Other as a fallback for niche platforms.

03

Paste & Fetch

Paste the link and click Fetch. LinkToDownload loads the video title and thumbnail so you can confirm it's the right video before downloading.

04

Download — done!

Click Download and the file saves with the original video title. MP4 files include real video and audio. MP3 files are extracted at the highest available bitrate.

Questions
Frequently asked questions
How do I download a YouTube video as MP4?
+
Paste the YouTube URL into the input box, make sure MP4 is selected in the Format toggle, then click Fetch → Download. The file saves with the original YouTube video title.
Can I download MP3 audio from YouTube?
+
Yes. Toggle the Format to MP3, paste the link and click Download. In Best mode, yt-dlp extracts and converts the audio to high-bitrate MP3 using ffmpeg. In Direct and Other modes it streams the best available audio format.
Which video platforms are supported?
+
LinkToDownload supports 1000+ sites including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, Vimeo, Reddit, Twitch, Dailymotion, SoundCloud, Bilibili and many more — powered by yt-dlp which adds new sites regularly.
Is LinkToDownload completely free?
+
Yes, 100% free. No account required, no watermarks, no subscription, and no ads injected into your downloads. Just paste and download.
Why does my MP4 only have audio and no video?
+
This happens when Direct or Other engine picks an audio-only stream. Switch to Best mode — it forces yt-dlp to download a real video stream and merge it with ffmpeg, so you always get proper video.
What is the maximum video quality?
+
Up to 4K (2160p) for platforms that offer it, such as YouTube. Use Best mode to get the highest available resolution. Quality depends on what the original video was uploaded in.
What does the server signal mean?
+
The signal in the top bar shows whether the download server is reachable. Green = online and ready. Red = server offline — start the local server and ngrok tunnel. Click the pill to recheck instantly. It also auto-checks every 15 seconds.
Can I install this as an app on my phone?
+
Yes! On Android, open Chrome, tap the menu and choose "Add to Home screen". On iPhone, open Safari, tap Share and choose "Add to Home Screen". The app icon will appear on your home screen.
